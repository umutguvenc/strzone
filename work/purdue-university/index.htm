Project

  • Client: Krach Institute of Tech Diplomacy at Purdue University
  • Type: Informational site
  • Year: 2022

Services

  • —Strategy
  • —Tech Consultancy
  • —Creative direction
  • —UX/Design
  • —Website

Challenge

Establish the digital communication for the Krach Institute and create a platform that will deliver targeted information to its diverse audience including US policy makers, foreign governments and business leaders.

Solution

Our solution was to create a platform that serves as the main resource hub, showcases the members and latest work of the organization, presents the most up-to-date news and events, and gives the flexibility to add new partnerships and future initiatives.

Result

The wide range of digital assets that we created for the Krach Institute contributed to better brand awareness and more efficient communication. We achieved a better reach, a growing number of engaged users, and established a platform that enabled integration with partner sites and the ability to add features such as online courses or have gated content.
